Getting Lost in a Familiar Place

Going on a walk can be a form of resistance. The streets are here for us to use and enjoy.


Dérive

The streets can be a liminal space. A place between other places. You go on them to get somewhere else - home, work, or a store. Usually we are only present on them in between spaces where we get or spend money.

Enter a Dérive. Treat the street as it's own destination. Go on an walk, without needing a place to get to. Let yourself drift and follow your feelings.
